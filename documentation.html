Variation

A variation holds the pricing and other data for a product. A product will have one or more variations. Due to record keeping, variations cannot be edited. In order to change a variation on a product, a new variation should be added and the old variation archived. A single variation would be a standard product with a single price. While a product with multiple variations would be something like an item of clothing that comes in different sizes.

Properties

descriptor

string

A short description of the product variation. Max 100 characters

price

number

Price of the item in cents. Must be an integer.

quantity

number

Quantity of the item available. -1 if no quantity

shipping

number

Cost of shipping the item in cents. Must be an integer

images

[string]

List of file names of the images associated with the particular variation

purchaseOption

string

How this variation is available. "ship" is purchase/shipping, "buy" is onlinebuying, but no shipping, "list" is no online purchase option.
